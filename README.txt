Match-Go
--------

This is an implementation in the Go language of the 'Match' program invented by:
van Casteren, M., & Davis, M. H. (2007). Match: A program to assist in matching the conditions of factorial experiments. Behavior Research Methods, 39(4), 973–978. https://doi.org/10.3758/BF03192992

The software is designed to automate the selection of matched subsets of items from larger candidate sets for use in factorial experimental designs.
